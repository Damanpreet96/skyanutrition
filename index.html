About Us

Skya Transdermic is a pioneering skincare and haircare brand that blends advanced transdermal technology with premium natural ingredients to deliver visible, lasting results. Founded in 2015, the company offers a diverse range of dermatologist-approved products, including serums, creams, and nutraceuticals, all crafted to meet international quality standards. With a strong presence in India and expanding exports to countries like Bangladesh, Nepal, and Sri Lanka, Skya Transdermic is committed to innovation, safety, and sustainability in every product. Their dedication to excellence has positioned them among the top cosmetic companies in India, trusted by skincare professionals and consumers alike.
